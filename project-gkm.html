Thanjai Manam (Fusion of Traditional and Contemporary From Thanjavur Style)

Thanjai Manam is one of our recent projects completed in December 2022. Our client's aim was to build temporary structure. We embraced this challenge with creative and resourceful approach.

Project Overview :

Our clientâ€™s aim was to build temporary structure that would capture essence of Thanjavur. To achieve this we utilized combination of customized metal sheet containers bamboo and concrete. To capture the essence and ambiance of Thanjavur, we incorporated Athangudi floor tiles in the main dining area. These traditional tiles add a touch of local culture and artistry, enhancing the overall aesthetic. Additionally, we themed the color palette in shades of yellow, maintaining authenticity and paying homage to the vibrant heritage of Thanjavur.

Project Highlights :

Athangudi Floor Tiles: To create authentic Thanjavur ambiance we incorporated traditional Athangudi floor tiles in main dining area.
Color Theme: Overall color theme was yellow. This maintained warm and inviting atmosphere that reflects rich cultural heritage of Thanjavur Metal Sheets.
(Customized Containers): Used for primary structure. This provided both durability and flexibility for temporary design.
Bamboo: Incorporated for its eco-friendliness and aesthetic appeal. Concrete: Used strategically to ensure stability and longevity while complementing other materials.
Thanjai Manam stands as testament to our ability to blend traditional elements with modern construction techniques. It creates spaces that are both functional and culturally resonant.
